AREA OF TRIANGLE

It refers to the amount of space that the triangle covers or takes up.Formula=1/2*b*h

AREA OF SQUARE

The space covered by the object. It is the region occupied by any shape.Formula=a*a

AREA OF RECTANGLE

It is the region occupied by a rectangle within its four sides or boundaries.Formula=l*b

PERIMETER OF TRIANGLE

The Perimeter of a triangle refers to the total length of its boundaries. Formula=s1+s2+s3

PERIMETER OF SQUARE

Perimeter of a square is the distance covered by its four sides. Formula=4*(a+a+a+a)

PERIMETER OF RECTANGLE

Perimeter of a rectangle is the total distance covered by its boundaries or the sides. Formula=2(a+b)

VOLUME OF CUBE

A cube is a solid three-dimensional figure, which has 6 square faces or sides. Formula=a*a*a

VOLUME OF CUBOID

The volume of a cuboid is given by the product of its dimensions. Formula=l*b*h

VOLUME OF SPHERE

The volume here depends on the diameter of the radius of the sphere. Formula=4/3 πr3

SURFACE OF CUBE

The sum of areas of all the faces of cube, that covers it. Formula=6a2

SURFACE OF CUBOID

A cuboid is a 3d figure or solid which has six rectangular sides called faces. Formula=2(lw + wh + lh)
